Easy Mashable Social Bar plugin is a social share plugin inspired by Mashable.com for Twitter, Facebook, Google+, LinkedIn, RSS and email subscription function.

== Description ==

This beautiful Easy Mashable Social Bar wordpress plugin is inspired from Mashable.com, coded by Syafz in partnership with Woot.my. Every setting of this plugin can be set on the widget page section. Just drag-and-drop the widget to the sidebar. If you are facing any problem with this widget or bug, please drop me a comment at the [Easy Mashable Social Bar plugin page](http://inspiredmagz.com/easy-mashable-social-bar-wordpress-plugin/ "Easy Mashable Social Bar plugin page").

A few notes about the sections above:

*   Automatically display the social share buttons. 
*   Display Facebook Like thumbnail on your blog sidebar.
*   Google +1 share option for each post. Easily visitors share your blog post.
*   Offer Feedburner email subscription option.
*   Show your LinkedIn and Google+ profile. 
*   Easily to customize the widget width and height.
*   You can change the background to any color you want.

For more information visit [Easy Mashable Social Bar plugin page](http://inspiredmagz.com/easy-mashable-social-bar-wordpress-plugin/ "Easy Mashable Social Bar plugin page")

== Installation ==

This section describes how to install the plugin and get it working.

e.g.

1. Download this Easy-Mashable-Social-Bar.zip file to your computer.
1. Go to Plugins and click Add New to upload and activate the plugin.
1. Then, go to Widgets section and drag-and-drop the Easy Mashable Social Bar to the sidebar.
1. Simply enter your Feedburner, Twitter, and Facebook ID in. Click Save.
